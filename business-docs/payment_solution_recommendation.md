## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Q: Do I need an LLC to use Stripe?
**A:** No! You can use Stripe as a sole proprietor with just your SSN. You'll file taxes under Schedule C.

### Q: How long until I receive payouts?
**A:** First payout takes 7-10 days. After that, payouts are every 2 business days automatically.

### Q: What if a client disputes a charge?
**A:** Stripe handles disputes professionally. You provide documentation (contract, emails) and Stripe mediates. With clear contracts, disputes are rare.

### Q: Can clients pay without a credit card?
**A:** Stripe supports credit cards, debit cards, and ACH bank transfers (for an additional 0.8% fee).

### Q: What about taxes?
**A:** Stripe reports your income to the IRS via 1099-K if you earn $20,000+. You'll report it on Schedule C. Consider using QuickBooks or Wave for bookkeeping.

### Q: Can I use Stripe AND Venmo?
**A:** Yes! Keep Venmo as an option for small, informal payments. Use Stripe for professional projects and subscriptions.
